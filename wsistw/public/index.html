Are you overwhelmed by the number of art exhibitions in your city? Would you like to randomly choose one to see, thereby getting you out of your comfort zone, and taking the onus for choosing a "decent" or "cool" show out of your hands entirely? Well, you are in luck!

WSISTW is an art exhibition randomizer inspired by wtfsigte.com (Where the Fuck Should I Go to Eat) and drawing on an external API.

This is a project by Siwin Lo
